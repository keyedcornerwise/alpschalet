Ledro Mountain Chalet

Description

Located in Ledro, Ledro Mountain Chalet has free WiFi, and guests can enjoy a seasonal outdoor swimming pool, barbecue facilities and a garden.

The chalet provides guests with a terrace, mountain views, a seating area, a flat-screen TV, a fully equipped kitchenette with a dishwasher and a microwave, and a private bathroom with bidet. A balcony with garden views is offered in all units.

An Italian breakfast is available each morning at Ledro Mountain Chalet.

The wellness area at the accommodation is comprised of a hot tub and a sauna.

Ledro Mountain Chalet provides a playground.

Riva del Garda is 19.3 km from the chalet. Villafranca Airport is 103 km from the property.
